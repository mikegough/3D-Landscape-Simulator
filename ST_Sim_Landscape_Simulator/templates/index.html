Welcome & Getting Started

The 3D Landscape Simulator is a web-based State-and-transition modelling framework powered by ST-Sim.

To get started with the 3D Landscape Simulator, first select an area of interest in the map. Then use the controls that appear on the left hand side to define the scenario you'd like to simulate.

When you are ready, push the run button to conduct a model run. Your results will appear on the right hand side of the screen in a series of charts designed to communicate the future composition of your landscape under the scenario you defined.
